Statutory Audit Services

Comprehensive statutory audit services ensuring compliance with Companies Act 2013 and regulatory requirements for transparent financial reporting

What is Statutory Audit?

Statutory Audit is a type of audit mandated by statute or law to ensure true and fair view of the books of accounts. Companies Act 2013 has made it compulsory for all companies to go for statutory audits to ensure that the financial reports are in order and comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

Our statutory audit services provide independent examination of your company's financial statements, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and transparency in financial reporting.

Our Statutory Audit Portfolio

Statutory Audit Applicability

  • All companies (Private Limited, One Person Company, Limited Company, Section 8 Company, Nidhi Company, Producer Company) irrespective of nature of business and sales turnover
  • All Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) with annual sales turnover exceeding Rs.40 lakhs or capital contribution exceeding Rs.25 lakhs

Audit Coverage

  • Financial statement examination
  • Internal control assessment
  • Compliance verification
  • Risk assessment and management
  • Fraud detection procedures
  • Regulatory compliance review

What is Tax Audit?

Tax audit is conducted to verify compliance with Income Tax Act 1961. It is mandatory for businesses with specific turnover limits and helps ensure accurate tax filing and compliance with tax regulations. Our tax audit services provide comprehensive examination of your books of accounts and tax computations.

We ensure that your tax audit is conducted efficiently with detailed documentation and professional reporting to meet all regulatory requirements.
