Indirect Tax Advisory Services (GST)

The introduction of Goods & Services Tax (GST) in India effective July 1, 2017 has been the most important change in the country’s Indirect Tax structure. The challenge at hand for businesses is to adapt to the new GST regime by understanding its various nuances. We at TFK & CO, understand and analyse the GST implications applicable to your business activity and conduct an impact analysis. A comprehensive report and possible alternatives are suggested for the most beneficial tax position for your business.
